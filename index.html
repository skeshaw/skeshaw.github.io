Biography

I am currently working in the algorithms team of Adobe AdCloud Search (prev. Adobe Media Optimizer), where I build models which manage ad spends for businesses. I graduated from IIT Kanpur in the summer of 2017, with a major in Mathematics and Scientific Computing and a minor in Artificial Intelligence.
My research interests include neural text generation tasks, which include neural machine translation (NMT) and abstractive summarization. I have worked on a variety of projects in machine learning, including summarization, object detection, and Bayesian matrix factorization. I have also worked on transfer learning, both in machine translation and in reinforcement learning.
Football occupies my weekends, and I also like to play FIFA from time to time.
